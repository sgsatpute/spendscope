## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no trial, no freemium gate. You enter your tools, you get your audit. The full results are on screen immediately. Email is optional — we ask for it after you've seen the results, not before.

**Q: How do you make money?**
A: SpendScope is built by Credex, which sells discounted AI infrastructure credits to startups. If your audit shows significant savings, we'll mention that Credex might be able to help. That's it. If your spend is already optimized, we tell you that too.

**Q: How current is the pricing data?**
A: We verify pricing against official vendor pages each week. Every number in the audit engine traces back to a real pricing URL — you can see our full sources in our public pricing data file. If a number is wrong, email us at data@spendscope.app and we'll fix it within 24 hours.

**Q: What does "seats" mean for API tools?**
A: For flat-rate tools (Cursor, Copilot, Claude Pro), seats = number of paying users. For API tools (Anthropic API direct, OpenAI API direct), just enter your total monthly spend — we'll audit that against usage optimization patterns, not per-seat logic.

**Q: Will you sell my email?**
A: No. Your email is used to send you your audit report and, if relevant, for Credex to follow up about credits. We don't share it, sell it, or use it for anything else. You can unsubscribe from any email we send.

## Supporting copy (below the form)

**Why does this exist?**

Most startups have no idea if they're overspending on AI. They signed up for Copilot Business when they had 3 engineers, added Cursor when a developer asked, and now they're paying for both without knowing if either is right-sized. There's no "Mint for AI tools." SpendScope is that.

**What does the audit check?**

For each tool: whether you're on the right plan for your team size, whether a cheaper plan from the same vendor would cover your needs, whether there's a substantially better alternative for your use case, and — for heavy API users — whether buying pre-purchased credits would be cheaper than retail.

**The fine print:**

We don't have access to your actual billing data. You enter what you pay; we audit it. Our pricing data is verified weekly but may lag by up to 7 days for pricing changes vendors don't announce publicly. This is a benchmark tool, not financial advice.
