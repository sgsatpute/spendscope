## Methodology note

For tools with annual billing, monthly equivalents are used in audit comparisons unless the tool only offers annual pricing (in which case the annual/12 rate is shown). When a plan shows "custom pricing," no automated recommendation is made — the audit notes it's likely worth checking with the vendor.
